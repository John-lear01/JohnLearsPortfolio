Altitude Chamber

As the lead design engineer, I designed and fabricated a testing environment capable of simulating altitudes from sea level to 5,000 meters. The system features a LabVIEW user interface and an Arduino Uno to adjust chamber pressure based on a single user-supplied input, allowing accurate cross-referencing of altimeter readings.
